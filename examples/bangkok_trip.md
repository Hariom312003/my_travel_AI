## 3. Itinerary Grid

### Day 1 — Historic Temple Trail & Chao Phraya River
* **Morning (09:00 - 11:30)**: **The Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew**
  * *Activity*: Explore the historic spiritual heart of Thailand, observing the Emerald Buddha temple.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2.5 hours. Dense crowds.
  * *Transport*: Chao Phraya Tourist Boat from Sathorn Pier.
* **Afternoon (13:30 - 15:30)**: **Wat Pho (Temple of the Reclining Buddha)**
  * *Activity*: Visit the giant reclining Buddha statue and walk the historic massage school pavilions.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2 hours.
  * *Transport*: Walking (10 minutes from Grand Palace).
* **Evening (18:00 - 21:00)**: **Asiatique The Riverfront**
  * *Activity*: Evening open-air mall dining, souvenir shopping, and riverside walk.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours.
  * *Transport*: Free shuttle boat from Saphan Taksin Pier.

---

### Day 2 — Modern Shopping & Culinary Streets
* **Morning (09:00 - 11:30)**: **Chatuchak Weekend Market**
  * *Activity*: Shop local handicrafts, clothes, and traditional wooden items in the largest weekend market.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2.5 hours.
  * *Transport*: BTS Skytrain to Mo Chit Station.
* **Afternoon (13:30 - 16:00)**: **Siam Paragon & Sea Life Bangkok**
  * *Activity*: Cool down in the luxury shopping complex and explore the ocean aquarium.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2.5 hours.
  * *Transport*: BTS Skytrain to Siam Station.
* **Evening (18:00 - 21:00)**: **Yaowarat Road (Chinatown Street Food)**
  * *Activity*: Dinner tour tasting traditional oyster omelets, crab fried rice, and sweet mango sticky rice.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours. Fast-paced walking.
  * *Transport*: MRT Metro to Wat Mangkon Station.

---

### Day 3 — Green Spaces & Floating Canals
* **Morning (09:00 - 11:30)**: **Taling Chan Floating Market**
  * *Activity*: Traditional canal boat ride, sampling food prepared directly on wooden longtail boats.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2.5 hours.
  * *Transport*: Taxi from central Bangkok.
* **Afternoon (13:30 - 15:30)**: **Lumpini Park**
  * *Activity*: Relaxed lakeside walk, watching giant monitor lizards and shade trees.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2 hours. Slow pacing.
  * *Transport*: MRT Metro to Silom Station.
* **Evening (18:00 - 21:00)**: **Mahanakhon SkyWalk**
  * *Activity*: Observe the panoramic sunset view of Bangkok's skyline from the glass floor sky deck.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours.
  * *Transport*: BTS Skytrain to Chong Nonsi Station.

---

## 4. Local Travel Notes & Tips
1. **Dress Code**: Grand Palace and temples require shoulders and knees to be covered.
2. **Scam Alert**: Ignore tuk-tuk drivers telling you temples are "closed today". They are open daily.
3. **Pacing Guardrail**: Bangkok is humid; carry bottled water and schedule rest breaks in air-conditioned malls.
