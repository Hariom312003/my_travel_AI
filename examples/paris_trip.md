## 3. Itinerary Grid

### Day 1 — Masterpieces of Art & Scenic Cruises
* **Morning (09:00 - 11:30)**: **The Louvre Museum**
  * *Activity*: View the Mona Lisa, Winged Victory, and grand renaissance wings.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2.5 hours. Dense crowds.
  * *Transport*: Metro Line 1 to Palais Royal - Musée du Louvre.
* **Afternoon (13:30 - 15:30)**: **Jardin des Tuileries**
  * *Activity*: Relaxed stroll through the historic gardens towards Place de la Concorde.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2 hours. Calm, relaxed pacing.
  * *Transport*: Walking (adjacent to the Louvre).
* **Evening (18:00 - 21:00)**: **Seine River Dinner Cruise**
  * *Activity*: Evening boat cruise observing glowing monuments (Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame) with a 3-course French dinner.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours.
  * *Transport*: Metro Line 9 to Alma-Marceau.

---

### Day 2 — Architectural Icons & High Views
* **Morning (09:00 - 11:30)**: **Eiffel Tower (Summit access)**
  * *Activity*: Climb to the highest deck for an expansive view of the city layout.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2.5 hours. Pre-booked ticket entry.
  * *Transport*: RER C Train to Champ de Mars Tour Eiffel.
* **Afternoon (13:30 - 15:30)**: **Musée d'Orsay**
  * *Activity*: Admire the world's largest collection of impressionist masterpieces inside a stunning historic railway station.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2 hours.
  * *Transport*: RER C Train to Musée d'Orsay.
* **Evening (18:00 - 21:00)**: **Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur**
  * *Activity*: Climb the cobblestone steps of the artists' hill and see the sunset view from the white basilica.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours. Uphill walking.
  * *Transport*: Metro Line 2 to Anvers.

---

### Day 3 — Royalty & Classic Avenues
* **Morning (09:00 - 12:00)**: **Palace of Versailles**
  * *Activity*: Walk the Hall of Mirrors and explore the vast royal gardens.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours. Long walk.
  * *Transport*: RER C Train to Versailles Château Rive Gauche.
* **Afternoon (13:30 - 15:30)**: **Jardin du Luxembourg**
  * *Activity*: Relax by the octagonal pond, watching model sailboats and classic statues.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 2 hours. Scenic, slow pacing.
  * *Transport*: RER B Train to Luxembourg.
* **Evening (18:00 - 21:00)**: **Arc de Triomphe & Champs-Élysées**
  * *Activity*: Climb the Arc de Triomphe for a view of the starry avenues, followed by dinner on the boulevard.
  * *Pacing & Duration*: 3 hours.
  * *Transport*: Metro Line 1 to Charles de Gaulle - Étoile.

---

## 4. Local Travel Notes & Tips
1. **Pre-book Tickets**: Eiffel Tower and Louvre tickets sell out weeks in advance. Do not rely on walk-up booths.
2. **Pickpocket Warning**: Be vigilant around crowded areas (specifically metro lines, Eiffel Tower, and Louvre entrance).
3. **Dining Hours**: Traditional Parisian restaurants serve lunch from 12:00 to 14:00 and dinner from 19:30 onwards.
